About Me

Hi, I’m Paula, a Polish portrait photographer based between Italy and Poland.

I’m passionate about capturing authentic emotions — the quiet, intimate, and powerful moments that reveal who we really are. My focus is on portraits of women, couples, and individuals, creating timeless images that feel natural and deeply personal.

When I’m not behind the camera, you’ll find me chasing soft light, connecting with people, and finding beauty in simplicity.
